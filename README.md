Complete Wellness Guide 🌿
Welcome! This guide will help you build sustainable wellness habits through nutrition, mindful 
movement, and daily practices that support your body naturally.
Daily Wellness Routine 🌿
Morning
• Start with warm lemon water to hydrate and support digestion
• Eat a balanced breakfast with protein, healthy fats, and whole grains
• Get 10-15 minutes of natural sunlight
• Take 5 minutes for deep breathing (in through nose for 4 counts, hold for 4, out for 4)
Midday
• Stay hydrated — aim for 8-10 glasses of water
• Move your body for 20-30 minutes (walk, stretch, light exercise)
• Eat a nutrient-dense lunch with vegetables, lean protein, and healthy carbs
Evening
• Wind down 30 minutes before bed
• Practice 5-10 minutes of meditation or breathing exercises
• Prioritize 7-9 hours of quality sleep
• Light dinner with easily digestible foods
Nutrition Foundation 🌿
The foundation of wellness is what you eat. These 5 recipes support your body's natural functions 
and work best when you make them part of your routine.
Recipe 1: Ginger & Turmeric Wellness Tea ☕
Ingredients:
• 1 cup hot water
• 1 teaspoon fresh grated ginger
• ½ teaspoon turmeric powder
• ½ teaspoon raw honey
• Squeeze of lemon juice
• Pinch of black pepper
Instructions:
1. Boil water and pour into a cup
2. Add ginger, turmeric, and black pepper
3. Steep for 5 minutes
4. Stir in honey and lemon juice
5. Drink while warm
Supports: Healthy digestion and natural inflammation response
Recipe 2: Detox Green Tea Blend 🌿
Ingredients:
• 1 cup hot water
• 1 green tea bag
• 1 tablespoon fresh mint leaves
• ½ lemon, squeezed
• 1 teaspoon raw honey (optional)
Instructions:
1. Steep green tea and mint in hot water for 3-4 minutes
2. Add lemon juice
3. Sweeten with honey if desired
4. Drink warm
Supports: Natural cleansing and sustained energy
Recipe 3: Balanced Breakfast Bowl 🌿
Ingredients:
• ½ cup rolled oats
• 1 cup unsweetened almond milk
• 1 banana, sliced
• 2 tablespoons raw almonds
• 1 tablespoon raw honey
• 1 teaspoon cinnamon
• Pinch of sea salt
Instructions:
1. Cook oats with almond milk for 5 minutes
2. Top with banana slices and almonds
3. Drizzle with honey and sprinkle cinnamon
4. Serve warm
Supports: Sustained energy and balanced blood sugar
Recipe 4: Rainbow Vegetable Bowl 🌿
Ingredients:
• 1 cup quinoa (cooked)
• ½ cup roasted sweet potato
• ½ cup steamed broccoli
• ¼ cup shredded carrots
• ¼ cup purple cabbage
• 4 oz grilled chicken or tofu
• 2 tablespoons tahini dressing (tahini + lemon + garlic)
Instructions:
1. Prepare quinoa according to package
2. Roast sweet potato at 400°F for 20 minutes
3. Steam broccoli for 4 minutes
4. Mix all vegetables and protein in a bowl
5. Drizzle with tahini dressing
Supports: Nutrient density, sustained fullness, and natural vitality
Recipe 5: Evening Recovery Soup 🌿
Ingredients:
• 4 cups low-sodium vegetable broth
• 1 cup diced carrots
• 1 cup diced celery
• 1 cup diced zucchini
• ½ cup diced tomatoes
• 2 cloves garlic, minced
• 1 teaspoon dried oregano
• Salt and pepper to taste
• Fresh parsley for garnish
Instructions:
1. Heat broth in a pot over medium heat
2. Add carrots, celery, and garlic
3. Simmer for 10 minutes
4. Add zucchini, tomatoes, and oregano
5. Simmer for another 8 minutes until vegetables are tender
6. Season with salt and pepper
7. Garnish with fresh parsley
Supports: Easy digestion and restful sleep preparation
Daily Movement 🌿
You don't need intense workouts. Consistency matters more than intensity.
Daily Movement Practices
• Morning: 10-minute walk or gentle stretching
• Midday: 20-30 minute walk outside (bonus: fresh air and sunlight)
• Evening: 5-10 minutes of gentle stretching or yoga
Weekly Goal
• Aim for 150 minutes of light to moderate movement per week
• This can be walking, stretching, dancing, or any activity you enjoy
• Listen to your body and move in ways that feel good
Mindfulness & Breathing 🌿
A calm mind supports a healthy body. These practices take just 5-10 minutes daily.
Simple Breathing Technique
1. Find a comfortable seated position
2. Breathe in through your nose for 4 counts
3. Hold for 4 counts
4. Exhale through your mouth for 4 counts
5. Repeat 5-10 times
When to use: Morning to start your day calm, or evening to wind down
Brief Meditation
1. Sit comfortably for 5 minutes
2. Close your eyes and focus on your breath
3. When your mind wanders, gently return focus to breathing
4. That's it — consistency matters more than perfection
Making It a Lifestyle 🌿
These practices work best when they become part of your daily routine, not something you do 
occasionally.
